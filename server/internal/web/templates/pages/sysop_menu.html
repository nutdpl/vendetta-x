main menu

What lives in each slot of the telnet/ssh main menu, its label, and the key that picks it. "-- empty --" leaves a slot blank. Hotkeys must be a single character and unique among the slots you enable; | and } aren't allowed in labels or hotkeys (they're reserved by the art renderer). Feature-gated commands (email, doors, voting, ...) still honor their on/off switch under settings no matter which slot they're bound to -- unbinding a slot here just hides it from the menu, it doesn't disable the feature.
