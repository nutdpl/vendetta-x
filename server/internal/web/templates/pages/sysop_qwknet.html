qwk networking

Join a QWK message network: on each exchange the board uploads its new local posts as a .REP to the hub and imports the hub's .QWK packet, so mapped boards carry the whole network's traffic. Exchanges run over FTP (what Synchronet hubs carrying DOVE-Net and friends serve). Schedule regular exchanges with a "QWK-net exchange" event -- an interval of 60 minutes is typical.
